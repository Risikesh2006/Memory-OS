Timeline – Open World Concept

An infinite open world timeline where your memories float freely in space.

  • Drag cards anywhere
  • Move freely in 2D space
  • Smooth physics & collisions
  • Zoom in / out
  • Search by year or time
  • Double click to enlarge
  • Glassmorphism cards
  • Organize into collections

How It Works

  1. 1. Memories appear as floating glass cards
  2. 2. Drag cards anywhere you like
  3. 3. Cards have soft collisions
  4. 4. Zoom and move around freely
  5. 5. Search any year or time to focus
  6. 6. Double click a card to see full details
  7. 7. Create collections and group memories

Card Information

  • Thumbnail (image / video)
  • Title
  • Date & Time
  • Location
  • Short Note / Description
  • Collection Tag

Interactions

Drag – Move card

Click – Select card

Double Click – Enlarge card

Scroll – Zoom in/out

Right Click – More options
